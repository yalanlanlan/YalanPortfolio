Yalan Wen’s work explores the subtle events that happen beyond the surface, finding the balance between simplicity and subtle philosophical interpretations. Her art incorporates programming, experimental music composition, visual and motion graphics. Born and raised in Taiwan, she developed her curiosity about art and science by observing nature. Her background in graphic design became the foundation of her visual language, which she continues to develop in the MFA Computer Arts program at the School of Visual Arts in New York City. By putting subjects in an unexpected context, she draws the relationship between external and internal ideas.
