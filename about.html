HI WORKS ABOUT CONTACT

The present we are in now is the continuation of the last one. I like to seek out those meaningful, unrepeatable and subtle moments, and I’m always passionate about learning new things.

Through art, I try to convey the feelings and moments I’ve captured. Through design, photography, coding and composing music, I communicate with myself, with others and with the world.
